Planning Rules – trains to Leyland Sidings
Trains to Leyland Sidings will stop on the Down Slow at Farington Jn. A 4 minute OP stop to be shown for the
driver to collect radio and prepare to propel towards Leyland Sidings.
Trains cannot access Leyland Sidings from Lostock Hall via the Up/Down Lostock Hall, due to there being no
signalled route.
