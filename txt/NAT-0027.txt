2.17 Summer Dated Services
      The standard period of operation of Summer Dated services is:

               Monday 13/05/2024 to Sunday 01/09/2024 inclusive

      Timetable Participants are encouraged to adopt these dates but may propose other dates for specific
      services where appropriate.



2.18 Autumn (Leaf-Fall) Timetables
      The standard period of operation of Autumn Dated services for ‘Leaf-Fall’ timetables are from the second
      Sunday in October:

               Sunday 12/10/2024 to Sunday 07/12/2024
