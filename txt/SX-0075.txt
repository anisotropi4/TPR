Front Train Working at Terminal locations:
In the event of Front Train Working with two (or more) loaded services a minimum of 5 minutes should be allowed
between departure of Front Train and Rear Train to allow indicators etc to be changed. Passenger trains that divide
en-route should not be “front trained”.
