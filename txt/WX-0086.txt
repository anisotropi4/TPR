Station Working Requirements
All trains that stop additionally at Surbiton (for Hampton Court Flower Show) are to be advertised 3 minutes later at
destination.

Restriction
An Up train cannot depart/pass Surbiton until after the previous Up train via the same line has passed Berrylands
Junction. This is because there is only one signal section between Surbiton and Berrylands Junction on both the
Up Fast and Up Slow lines.
