EA1430 EAST SUFFOLK JUNCTION TO OULTON BROAD NORTH JUNCTION

Ipswich Signal CO348

Standage Length
Up East Suffolk Line towards East Suffolk Junction & Ipswich standing at Ipswich Signal CO348
                                                                                                   46 SLUs
clear of Boss Hall Junction

Restrictions
Trains longer than 46 SLUs stopped at Ipswich Signal CO348 will foul Boss Hall Junction. In this circumstance
headway at Boss Hall Junction applies between the departure time for the first train departing Ipswich Signal
CO348 and the passing time of the second train at Boss Hall Junction.
