Barassie Junction
Trains to Barassie Yard from Irvine require ½ minute OP stop at Barassie Station for approach control.
Trains to Barassie Yard from Kilmarnock / Caledonian Paper require ½ minute OP stop at Barassie Junction for
approach control.
Train cannot reverse in DPL to run towards Irvine if there is a train on the line from Kilmarnock or Caledonian
Paper.


Barassie Yard Run Round

Junction Margins

Note
Applies to Sidings 5 & 6 only on Network Rail infrastructure

Prestwick International Airport

Restriction
Down trains cannot arrive at this Station until at least 2 minutes after preceding train departs Prestwick Town.
