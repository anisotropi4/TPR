6.2 Timing of Light Locomotives
It is a general Principal that all light locomotive movements will be timed. Any exceptions to this must be agreed by
the appropriate Operational Planning Manager.

Light Engine movements to be treated as passenger trains when applying margins/allowances where there is
a freight/passenger difference.
