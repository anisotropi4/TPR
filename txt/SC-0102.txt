Restrictions

Trains cannot be timed to stand or reverse between Newton and Newton West Junction in either direction.
Trains from Uddingston cannot be timed to stand or reverse between Newton East Junction and Newton.
Trains from Newton are restricted to 282m.
Trains can reverse on Up Hamilton to South of Newton but subject to a maximum length of 535m.
Turnback Siding is subject to a maximum length of 125m.
