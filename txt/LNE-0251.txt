LN808 DORE STATION JUNCTION TO EARLES SIDINGS (EXCL.)

Dore & Totley
Planning Note
Up trains requiring pathing stops are to have the stop shown at Dore & Totley and not Dore Station Junction as
Signal DE5124 protecting the junction is at Dore & Totley station.

No allowances to be applied in either direction between the station and Dore Station Junction.
