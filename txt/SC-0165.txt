Gorton

All trains must stop for token exchange purposes, unless a Long Section Token has been issued at the
previous Token Exchange Point.

Restriction
Trains can not pass at Gorton under normal methods of working.

Both ends of the loop are controlled by groundframes which are set and locked for main line under normal
operating conditions.
