Eastleigh Signal E26

Length Restrictions
A train standing at Signal E26 on the Down Slow which exceeds 612m / 95 SLU in length (exclusive of standback
allowance) will foul Allbrook Junction. Therefore, junction margins at Allbrook must be based on the train’s
departure time at Signal E26 if over length.
