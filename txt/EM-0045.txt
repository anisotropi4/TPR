5.3 Junction Margins and Station Planning Rules
Line of Route
The definition for Junction Margins and Station Planning Rules is listed in Section 1.6 – 1.10 of the National TPRs.

All times shown are in minutes. Where adjustments to sectional running times are shown, the value must be added to
the normal SRTs shown in Bplan. Negative adjustments are specially identified.

Minimum station allowances are the minimum practical for a particular type of stock. These are shown with
exceptions being listed by line of route where applicable.

Light Engine movements, postal and test trains to be treated as passenger trains when applying margins/allowances
where there is a passenger/freight differential.
