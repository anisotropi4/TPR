Restriction
All trains arriving into Grangemouth Run Round must have a stop of 2 minutes at Fouldubs Junction for Shunting
instruction.
Where a train is over 93 SLU or both No 2 and 3 loop are occupied a 30 minute dwell is required due to longer run
round move.

Restriction
