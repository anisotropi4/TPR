Freight Restrictions
Freight trains may recess in Platforms 3 and 4 at Ashford International provided there is no requirement for the
driver to exit the cab (e.g. to change ends)

Reversals at Ashford International
Trains should not be planned to reverse on the Up Fast (UML) at Ashford International as this is not permissible
due to the track layout and position of the pointwork. Reversals on the Down Fast (DML) are permitted.
