Cambridge


Rolling Stock Restrictions – Class 387
Due to signal sighting issues at signal CA162 at the south end of Cambridge Platform 4, trains formed of 12-car
Class 387 rolling stock may only be planned to use Platform 4 when travelling in the Down (northbound direction).
A train formed of 12-car Class 387 rolling stock may be split in Platform 4 providing that both portions then depart
northbound.

Splitting and Coupling of trains permitted         In all platforms
