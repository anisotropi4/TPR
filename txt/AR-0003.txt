These represent the Amended Final Timetable Planning Rules (the “Final Rules”) for the
Subsidiary 2024 timetable in accordance with Part D of the Network Code, Condition D2.2.7.

As per Condition D2.2.8 of Part D of the Network Code, any Timetable Participant

accordance with Network Code Part D Condition D2.2.8 (a) and (b).
