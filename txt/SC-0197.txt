Note
Method of Working

One train working beyond Kittybrewster Junction to Waterloo Goods. For trains towards Waterloo Goods the
following method of working applies:

Trains arrives Kittybrewster Junction Arrival Line; Locomotive run round takes place via Rounding Line and
attaches to south end. 20 minutes to be allowed for this movement, then train departs to Waterloo Goods.

For trains exiting Waterloo Goods the following method of working applies:

Trains arrives Kittybrewster Junction Arrival Line; Locomotive run round takes place via Rounding Line and attaches
to south end. 20 minutes to be allowed for this movement, then train departs to Berryden Junction.
