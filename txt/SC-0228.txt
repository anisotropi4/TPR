5.5 Timing Allowances
All allowances shown are in minutes.
 LH/HST,22x       Refers to non-freight locomotive hauled trains, High Speed Trains (two Class 43 locomotives with
 & 80X            coaching stock) and Classes 220/221/222 & 80X
 MU               Refers to all forms of multiple unit, whether diesel or electric as well as to light locomotives. #
                  However, Scotrail EMU traction includes 5% allowance for Engineering purposes and therefore
                  the “E” allowances shown do not apply to these services.
 FRT              Refers to locomotive hauled freight trains

SX Daytime allowances apply at all times except where specified differently in Sections 5.5.2, 5.5.3, 5.5.4, 5.5.5 and
5.5.6. The locations at which allowances are included within a Train Slot may vary. The total allowance included
within a Train Slot will not exceed the maximum value allowed.
All allowances are indicative for the Final Principal Rules and are subject to change.
E refers to engineering allowance
P refers to performance allowances
