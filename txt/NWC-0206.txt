Planning Note
Trains standing at signal SP6610 on the Up Soho that are longer than 386 meters/60SLUs (exclusive of stand-
back allowance) will foul Soho East Jn. Therefore, junction margins at Soho East Jn must be based on trains’
departure time at Soho South Jn if over this length.
